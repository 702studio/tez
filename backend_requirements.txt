Email Forwarding for Forms:

To ensure the newsletter subscription and contact form submissions are received,
server-side email forwarding or processing is required.

- Form submissions from `index.html` (Newsletter and Contact sections) are intended to be sent from `support@702.studio`.
- These emails should be forwarded to or made accessible at `tolgaozisik@gmail.com`.

This setup is external to the static `index.html` file and needs to be configured at the email hosting or server level for `702.studio`.
